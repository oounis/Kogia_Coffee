# Legal & Compliance

> Tunisia-specific — confirm current requirements with a local accountant/lawyer. This is a checklist, not legal advice.

## To open (category `LEGAL`)
- [ ] **Business registration** (patente / registre de commerce)
- [ ] **Food-handling / hygiene permit** (selling consumable coffee + spices)
- [ ] **Tax ID** (matricule fiscal) + VAT registration if applicable
- [ ] **Municipal license** to operate the shop at the location
- [ ] Signage permit (if required by the municipality/medina)
- [ ] Insurance (premises + liability)

## Product / labelling
- [ ] Ingredient labelling per Tunisian food law (we already list ingredients — good).
- [ ] Net weight + best-before + batch on every bag.
- [ ] Allergen note if relevant (sesame in Marrakech Spice).

## E-commerce
- [ ] Terms & conditions + returns policy on the website
- [ ] Privacy note for customer data
- [ ] Online payment / delivery compliance

Indicative cost of licenses & procedures: **300 – 800 DT** (from earlier study).
