# Maintenance

Keep the equipment and space in top condition — a coffee shop lives or dies on hygiene and a working machine.

## Coffee / coin machine
- **Daily:** clean group/brewer, empty drip tray, wipe hoppers, descale check.
- **Weekly:** deep clean hoppers when rotating blends, backflush, check grinder burrs.
- **Monthly:** descale, service coin/token mechanism, calibrate grind.
- **Quarterly:** professional service. Log under `MAINT`.

## Roaster & grinder
- Clean chaff after every roast session.
- Replace grinder burrs per usage (track kg ground).

## Shop
- Daily: floors, counter, tasting bar, cups sanitised.
- Spice jars: refill from sealed stock, never top-up stale (rotate FIFO).
- Pest control + food-safety log (see Legal).

## Logs
- Keep a simple maintenance log (date / task / cost / category `MAINT`).
- Spare parts & cleaning supplies tracked under `MAINT`.
