# Brand Identity

## Name
**Kogia Coffee** (part of the Kogia family). Shop sub-line: *Djerba*.

## Tagline options
1. **"Roasted traditions, ground fresh."** ← recommended
2. "Taste a tradition."
3. "The spice of Djerba, in every cup."

## Logo direction
- A simple **coffee bean + spice / sun motif** in brushed brass on espresso brown.
- Works in one colour (for stamping bags, cups, brass plates).
- Arabic + Latin lockup: **كوجيا** / **KOGIA**.

## Colour palette
Same system as the shop & website (see `../ambiance_and_decor/Shop_Ambiance.md`): cream `#F4ECE0`, roast brown `#3B2415`, saffron amber `#C8741A`, olive `#7A8450`, brass `#B8893E`.

## Typography
- **Display/headings:** an elegant serif (e.g. *Playfair Display* / *Cormorant*) — premium, editorial.
- **Body/UI:** a clean humanist sans (e.g. *Inter* / *Poppins*).
- **Arabic:** a warm naskh (e.g. *Tajawal* / *Cairo*).

## Voice
Warm, confident, honest. We name our ingredients and grams. No fake "secret family" mystique — the proof is in the taste and the transparency.
