# Kogia Coffee — Company Profile

## Vision
Make Djerba's most distinctive **traditional coffee powder** — authentic spiced blends you can't buy in a supermarket — and sell them both in a small experience-driven shop and online across Tunisia.

## What we sell
**Coffee powder (poudre / بودرة)** in sealed bags. The product is a *blend*: premium coffee combined with roasted barley, roasted chickpeas, and aromatic spices (cardamom, coriander/tabil, orange peel, rosebuds…). Different blends = different taste profiles (aromatic, spicy, light, sweet, bold).

## Business model
Two revenue engines, one purpose — **sell the bag**:

1. **The shop (experience → conversion)**
   - A **coin-operated coffee machine**: insert coin → get a fresh cup. Low-friction trial.
   - A **tasting corner**: sit, taste the menu blends for free/cheap, get guided to the blend that fits your taste.
   - The point of both is to convert a taster into a **powder buyer** (take-home bags).
2. **The website (reach → recurring)**
   - Online store ships bags Tunisia-wide.
   - Owner dashboard tracks the money.

## Positioning
- **Premium but local** — Djerba-roasted, authentic recipes, elegant packaging.
- **Transparent** — we tell you exactly what's in each blend (grams, origin, roast).
- **Instagrammable** — the shop and packaging are designed to be photographed and shared.
