## Packaging (category `PACK`)
- Kraft stand-up pouches (250 g / 500 g / 1 kg) + valves
- Labels, blend color bands, gift sleeves, QR stickers

## Equipment (category `EQUIP`)
- Roaster, grinder, coin/token machine, scales, sealer, shelving, dallah & cups

## Supplier tracking
Keep a `suppliers/` sheet: supplier · item · price · MOQ · lead time · contact.
**Action:** lock 2 sources per critical item (beans, cardamom, pouches) to avoid stockouts.
