# ☕ Kogia Coffee

> Premium traditional coffee **powder** (poudre) — roasted blends, ground fresh, sold by the bag.
> A small boutique shop in **Djerba, Tunisia**, with a self-service coffee machine, a tasting corner, and an online store.

This repository is organized like a **company**, one folder per **department**. Each department owns its own documents, data, and assets.

| # | Department | What lives here |
|---|------------|-----------------|
| 01 | **Executive** | Company profile, vision, business model, legacy docs |
| 02 | **Products & R&D** | The coffee **menu**, blend **recipes**, taste profiles |
| 03 | **Operations** | Shop layout, the **coin machine**, the **tasting corner**, maintenance |
| 04 | **Finance** | Expense categories, the **pricing model**, budgets |
| 05 | **Marketing** | Brand identity, **shop ambiance & decor**, social media, packaging |
| 06 | **HR** | Staff roles, uniforms, staff items |
| 07 | **Procurement** | Suppliers, raw materials (beans, spices, packaging) |
| 08 | **Facilities** | Rent, decoration, fit-out |
| 09 | **Technology / Website** | The two-sided **e-commerce website** (storefront + owner dashboard) |
| 10 | **Legal & Compliance** | Licenses, permits, food-safety |

## The concept in one line
Sell **the powder**, not the cup. The machine + tasting corner exist to make people *taste* the blends → then **buy the bag to take home**.

## The website (department 09)
- **Client side** — beautiful storefront to browse blends and buy bags online.
- **Owner side** — private dashboard for Othman to track **expenses by category** and see sales.
- Open `09_Technology_Website/website/index.html` (storefront) or `dashboard.html` (owner).
