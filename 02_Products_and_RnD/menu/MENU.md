# ☕ Kogia Coffee — The Menu

Six house blends, each a clear taste profile. Sold as **powder** in 250 g / 500 g / 1 kg bags.
At the tasting corner, customers try, then buy the bag.

| # | Blend | Arabic | Profile | Roast | Best for |
|---|-------|--------|---------|-------|----------|
| 01 | **Djerbi Signature** | الجربية | Aromatic & rounded | Medium-dark | The everyday house cup |
| 02 | **Ethiopian Classic** | الكلاسيكية | Clean & coffee-forward | Light-medium | Purists who want more coffee |
| 03 | **Khaleeji Gold** | الذهبية | Light & floral | Light | Cardamom & saffron lovers |
| 04 | **Marrakech Spice** | الحارة | Spicy & warming | Medium-dark | Winter, bold-spice drinkers |
| 05 | **Sweet Dunes** | الحلوة | Naturally sweet | Medium | Sugar-free sweet tooth, gentle |
| 06 | **Sahara Ginger** | الصحراوية | Bold & zesty | Dark | Strong, energizing ginger kick |

## How to read the shelf
- Each bag shows: **profile**, **roast**, **ingredient list (with grams)**, **net weight**, **roast/pack date**.
- A small **taste-meter** on the label: Body ▓▓▓▓░ · Spice ▓▓▓░░ · Sweetness ▓▓░░░ · Aroma ▓▓▓▓▓.

## Tasting-corner flow
1. "How do you like your coffee — light, sweet, spicy, or strong?"
2. Pour the matching blend from the machine / dallah.
3. Hand them the matching bag. Done.

## At the coin machine
Stock **2–3 rotating blends** in the machine (e.g. Djerbi + Sweet Dunes + Marrakech Spice). Insert coin → fresh cup → "liked it? the bag is on the shelf behind you."
