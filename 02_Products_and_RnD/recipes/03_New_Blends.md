# New Blends — engineered variations (R&D)

Research found that almost all "recipes" online are *brewing* recipes, not powder blends. So these are **engineered from our two house blends** by adjusting the spice/grain balance toward a clear taste profile — each grounded in a real tradition. Formulas are starting points to dial in during tasting.

---
## Blend 03 — "Khaleeji Gold" · Light & Floral (الذهبية)
*Inspired by Gulf gahwa (light roast + heavy cardamom + saffron).*

**Profile:** light roast, pale, floral, very aromatic, low bitterness.

| Ingredient | Amount (per 1 kg coffee) |
|---|---|
| Coffee — **light roast** Arabica | 1000 g |
| Cardamom | **90 g** (heavy) |
| Saffron | **2 g** |
| Clove | 5 g |
| Rosebuds | 30 g |
| *No barley, no chickpea* | — keep it clean & light |

**Dial knob:** more cardamom → more aromatic; pinch more saffron → more "golden".

---
## Blend 04 — "Marrakech Spice" · Spicy & Warming (الحارة)
*Inspired by Moroccan six-spice coffee.*

**Profile:** dark, bold, warming, peppery-sweet spice.

| Ingredient | Amount (per 1 kg coffee) |
|---|---|
| Coffee — medium-dark | 1000 g |
| Cinnamon | 40 g |
| Ginger (dried) | 30 g |
| Nutmeg | 10 g |
| Star anise | 10 g |
| Black pepper | 5 g |
| Sesame (toasted) | 20 g |

**Dial knob:** more ginger → spicier/zestier; less pepper → softer.

---
## Blend 05 — "Sweet Dunes" · Naturally Sweet (الحلوة)
*Sweetness without sugar — from grains, cinnamon and date.*

**Profile:** smooth, mellow, naturally sweet, low acidity, very stomach-friendly.

| Ingredient | Amount (per 1 kg coffee) |
|---|---|
| Coffee — medium | 1000 g |
| Roasted barley | 250 g (more → sweeter, smoother) |
| Cinnamon | 30 g |
| Date powder (تمر مطحون) | 40 g |
| Cardamom | 20 g |
| Orange peel | 30 g |

**Dial knob:** more barley + date → sweeter & lighter in caffeine.

---
## Blend 06 — "Sahara Ginger" · Bold & Zesty (الصحراوية)
*Yemeni-style ginger-forward (qishr inspiration).*

**Profile:** strong, dark, sharp ginger kick, energizing.

| Ingredient | Amount (per 1 kg coffee) |
|---|---|
| Coffee — dark | 1000 g |
| Ginger (dried) | 60 g (dominant) |
| Cinnamon | 20 g |
| Clove | 10 g |
| Cardamom | 20 g |

**Dial knob:** more ginger → hotter; add chili flake pinch for a daring seasonal edition.

## Taste-tuning cheat sheet (for the tasting corner)
| Want it more… | Add | Remove |
|---|---|---|
| Aromatic | cardamom, rosebuds | barley |
| Spicy / warming | ginger, cinnamon, pepper | — |
| Sweet / smooth | barley, date, more roast | spice |
| Light | light roast, less grain | barley, chickpea |
| Bold / strong | dark roast, ginger, clove | rosebuds |
| Stomach-friendly | pomegranate peel, barley | pepper, heavy clove |
