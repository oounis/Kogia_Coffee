# Blend 01 — "Djerbi" Signature (الخلطة الجربية)

**This is the house blend** — the authentic Tunisian recipe (source: owner's original note, kept at `_original_signature_note_TN.txt`).

**Taste profile:** Aromatic & rounded · medium-dark roast · warming, slightly sweet-spiced, smooth on the stomach.

## Formula (per 1 kg coffee base)
| Ingredient | Amount | Roast note |
|---|---|---|
| Premium coffee beans (كعب) | **1000 g** | base |
| Roasted barley (شعير) | **200 g** | roast until brown→black, well-done, **not burnt** |
| Coriander / tabil (تابل) | **200 g** | roasted gently (بالقدي) |
| Roasted chickpeas (حمص) | **200 g** | roasted well (محمص بارشا) |
| Cardamom (هيل) | **50 g** | roasted |
| Orange peel (قشور برتقال) | **50 g** | roasted |
| Rosebuds (شوش ورد) | **50 g** | natural |
| Pomegranate peel (قشور رمان) | *optional* | roasted — good for digestion |

**Total batch:** ~1.75 kg blend (before optional pomegranate).
**Coffee ratio:** ~57% coffee / 43% roasted grains + botanicals.

## Method (summary)
1. Roast each component **separately** to its own target (barley darkest, spices lightest).
2. Cool fully.
3. Combine to the weights above, then **grind together** to a fine traditional grind.
4. Rest 24h, then pack in sealed bags (see Packaging).

> ⚠️ Roasting discipline is the whole game: barley well-roasted but never burnt, spices gentle. Burnt barley = bitter blend.
