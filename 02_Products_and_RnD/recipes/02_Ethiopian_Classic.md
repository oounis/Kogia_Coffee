# Blend 02 — Ethiopian Classic (الخلطة الكلاسيكية)

**Taste profile:** Cleaner & coffee-forward · lighter than the Signature · bright with a light spice lift. For drinkers who want **more coffee, fewer fillers**.

## Formula (per ~1 kg coffee base)
| Ingredient | Amount |
|---|---|
| Coffee beans — **100% Arabica** | **900 g** |
| Cardamom (هيل) | **50 g** |
| Clove (قرنفل) | **10 g** |
| Cinnamon (قرفة) | **10 g** |
| Orange peel (قشور برتقال) | to taste |

**Coffee ratio:** ~93% coffee. Minimal grain, so it tastes like premium spiced coffee rather than a traditional "mix".

## Method
1. Use a **lighter roast** on the Arabica to keep brightness.
2. Roast spices gently; keep clove/cinnamon modest so they lift, not dominate.
3. Grind together, rest, pack.

> Position this as the **"pure"** end of the menu — pairs with the premium 37 DT/kg bean.
