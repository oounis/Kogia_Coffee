# Facilities Department

## Rent (category `RENT`)
- Target: small unit in a **high-footfall / tourist** area of Djerba (medina, near beach hotels, or souk).
- Indicative monthly rent: **500 – 1,500 DT** (confirm per location).
- Budget a **deposit** (often 2–3 months) at signing.

## Fit-out / decoration (category `EQUIP` + `MKT` for signage)
Indicative initial fit-out (from earlier study): **3,000 – 8,000 DT**.
